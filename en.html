About Us

A passion for the authentic flavors of Sardinia

Sara Mandas

Co-founder and Business Development Manager

Alessandro Collu

Co-founder and Guardian of Sardinian Tradition

With deep knowledge of the island's culture and traditions, he contributes to selecting products that express Sardinia's authenticity and its bond with the land.

Every product is a testament to quality, dedication and love for this wonderful island.

Our Products

Authentic flavors from Sardinian tradition, from bottarga to truffle specialties

Mullet Bottarga

A product made through the careful selection of grey mullet roe sacs, followed by salting and drying.

Amber color Bold, savory flavor Slightly bitter aftertaste

Use: Grated over dishes, served in very thin slices with olive oil, or added to salads.

Cuttlefish Ink

Known for its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant and antibacterial properties, it is the defensive ink of cephalopods, extracted and transformed into a gastronomic ingredient.

High gastronomic value Low calorie Rich in minerals

Use: Pasta seasoning, or mixed with flour for black pizzas and breads.

Bottarga Cream

A refined cream where mullet roe, olive oil, mullet bottarga and anchovies come together in perfect harmony.

Taste of the sea Natural ingredients Versatile

Use: Sauce for first courses, or spread on crostini.

Black Truffle Cream

A pure, intense cream made with ground summer black truffle and Sardinian extra virgin olive oil.

100% pure truffle Bestseller Intense flavor

Use: Perfect with tagliolini and fresh pasta, eggs, risotto, meat, fresh cheeses, carasau bread, raw fish and prawns.
